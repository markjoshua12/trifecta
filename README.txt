Instructions:
-------------------------------

A,D    to move
W,S    to aim up/down
D      to crouch / fall down faster
SPACE  to jump
SHIFT  to sprint
J      to shoot
K      to dash

-,=    zoom in/out
F10    toggle fullscreen
ENTER  to type (for whatever reason)
